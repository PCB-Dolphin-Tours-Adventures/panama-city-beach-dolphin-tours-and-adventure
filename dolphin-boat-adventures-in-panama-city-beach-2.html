Frequently Asked Questions

Why do dolphins jump?

The dolphins do this to get a bird's-eye view of the ocean and see what happens above sea level. Dolphins jump to see the weather or identify threats.

What do you wear to a dolphin encounter?

After selecting your dolphin swim experience you will need to wear comfortable clothing as well as a swimsuit. For women, we recommend full-body suits and for men, shorts. You should also avoid wearing necklaces, bracelets and watches.

How does it feel to touch a dolphin?

The skin of a dolphin is rubbery and smooth. They are smooth because their skin must be able to glide through water smoothly without dragging. The skin of dolphins is sensitive and has many nerve endings. The skin of humans also has a large number of nerve endings, particularly on the face and hands.

What season is best to watch dolphins?

November is the peak month for dolphin migration. Even killer whales can be seen during this period. December is the month to watch dolphins in San Diego during the gray whales journey from Alaska down to Baja California.
